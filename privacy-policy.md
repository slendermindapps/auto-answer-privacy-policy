**Effective Date:** November 17, 2025  
**Last Updated:** November 17, 2025

## Introduction

Auto Answer ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App accesses, uses, and protects your information.

---

## Data Collection

Auto Answer accesses the following permissions and data on your device:

### 1. Contacts Permission
- **Purpose:** To identify incoming callers and determine which calls to auto-answer
- **Usage:** The App reads your contact list to match incoming phone numbers with saved contacts
- **Storage:** Contact data is processed locally on your device only

### 2. Phone State Permission
- **Purpose:** To detect incoming calls and manage call states
- **Usage:** The App monitors when calls are received to trigger auto-answer functionality
- **Storage:** Call state information is processed in real-time and not stored

### 3. Answer Phone Calls Permission
- **Purpose:** To automatically answer incoming calls from your contacts
- **Usage:** The App uses this permission to accept calls without manual interaction
- **Storage:** No call data is stored or logged

### 4. Modify Audio Settings Permission
- **Purpose:** To activate speakerphone when answering calls (optional feature)
- **Usage:** The App adjusts audio routing to enable hands-free calling
- **Storage:** Audio preferences are stored locally in app settings

---

## How We Use Your Data

**All data processing happens locally on your device.** Auto Answer does NOT:

- ❌ Transmit any data to external servers
- ❌ Upload contacts, call logs, or any personal information to the cloud
- ❌ Share your data with third parties
- ❌ Use analytics, tracking, or advertising services
- ❌ Collect usage statistics or crash reports
- ❌ Require internet connection or access network data

**What the App DOES:**
- ✅ Process contact information locally to identify callers
- ✅ Store user preferences (settings) locally on your device
- ✅ Auto-answer calls based on your configured settings
- ✅ Operate completely offline

---

## Data Storage and Retention

### Local Storage Only
All data remains on your device and is stored using Android's standard app storage mechanisms:

- **Contacts:** Read from your device's contact database (not copied or stored separately)
- **Settings:** Stored in app's local SharedPreferences
- **Premium Status:** Stored locally with Google Play Billing purchase token

### Data Deletion
All app data is automatically deleted when you:
- Uninstall the app
- Clear the app's data in Android Settings → Apps → Auto Answer → Storage → Clear Data

## Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

### Permission Management
You can revoke any permission at any time:
1. Open Android Settings
2. Navigate to Apps → Auto Answer → Permissions
3. Toggle off any permission you wish to revoke

**Note:** Revoking the Contacts or Phone State permissions will prevent the app from functioning.

### Data Access
You can view what data the app stores:
1. Open Android Settings
2. Navigate to Apps → Auto Answer → Storage
3. View app data size and clear if desired

### Account Deletion
To completely remove all data:
1. Uninstall Auto Answer from your device
2. All local data will be permanently deleted
